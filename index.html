About Me

I'm a sophomore at the College of William & Mary pursuing a major in Computational and Applied Mathematics and Statistics (CAMS). I'm passionate about quantitative analysis, and I love applying my skills in data analytics, applied mathematics, and econometrics to solve interesting problems that I've found! Currently, I’m working as a Research Analyst on the China Development Finance Program team at AidData—a research lab at William & Mary. In my free time, I enjoy reading, watching soccer, baking, and playing board games!
